ABOUT US

History

ATM Breads and Pastries is owned by Christelle Berboso Mangalindan. ATM Breads and Pastries started out as a simple Sari-Sari store under the management of Christelle's grandparents. When they passed away, Christelle inherited the Sari Sari Store business and is now currently managing it. When she learned how to make breads and pastries, she established ATM Breads and Pastries as an extension business alongside the pre-existing Sari Sari Store.
